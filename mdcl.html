How to Scan

Step 1: Open WhatsApp on your phone
Step 2: Tap Settings (or Menu) → Linked Devices
Step 3: Tap Link a device
Step 4: Point your phone at this screen to scan the QR
If the session is not connected, click Reset Session to load a new QR code.
